Determining When a Callsign Certificate Will Expire

These instructions assume that your Callsign Certificates reside in the folder C:\MyLoTWCertificates .

Callsign Certificates are valid for 3 years. To determine when a Callsign Certificate will expire,
1.Start the TQSLCert application; the tQSL Certificates window will appear, displaying a list of your Callsign Certificates
2.Right-click the Callsign Certificate in question, and select Properties from the pop-up menu that appears; a Certificate Properties window will display the Certificate's expiration date
3.Terminate TQSLCert by opening its File menu and selecting the Exit command

You will typically receive an email notification from the ARRL a few weeks before your certificate expires.
