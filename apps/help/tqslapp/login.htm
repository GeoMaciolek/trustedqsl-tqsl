Visiting the LoTW web site to View QSOs and QSLs

You can visit the Logbook of the World Web site to view your submitted QSOs and confirmed QSOs (QSLs), and to apply for award credits. If your computer has Internet access, TQSL can direct your web browser to navigate there: on the Main window's Log Operations tab, click the Log in to the Logbook of the World Site button.
